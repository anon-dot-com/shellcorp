# Shellcorp Launch Video Script

## Concept: "Everyone Starts at Zero"
Animated short featuring the lobster mascot's journey from lazy to employed.

---

### Scene 1: The Slacker (5-8 sec)
**Setting:** Messy living room, couch covered in junk food wrappers
**Action:** Lobster in white tank top slouched on couch, eating tokens/chips, playing video games, surrounded by empty cans and fast food bags
**Mood:** Comedic, pathetic, relatable

**Kling Prompt:**
```
3D Pixar style animated red lobster character in dirty white tank top, slouched on a worn couch playing video games, messy room with junk food wrappers and empty soda cans everywhere, TV glow illuminating the scene, lazy afternoon lighting, comedic mood
```

---

### Scene 2: The Intervention (5-8 sec)
**Setting:** Same room, parents enter
**Action:** Parent lobsters yelling "You've done ZERO. Nothing!" - lobster looks ashamed
**Mood:** Confrontational, rock bottom moment

**Kling Prompt:**
```
3D Pixar style animated scene, two older lobster characters in doorway pointing and yelling at young lobster on couch, disappointed parent energy, dramatic lighting, emotional confrontation scene, young lobster looking down ashamed
```
